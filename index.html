Our Beliefs

Vision And Mission

Vision

We at Animaux seek to create a world where animals live free from unjust suffering so that we can move the world of animals and make the world a lot kinder to them.

Vision

We want to be agents of sustainable change across the globe as regards saving stray animals, animals undergoing cruelty, malnutrition and other harmful vices.

Vision

We are cutting off dangers so we can see our ecosystem safe for life by providing a means to the end of threat on the life of animals round the globe and our environment.

Mission

To be an organization committed to continually effecting positive change at every point in time and as well helping the world be a better place each moment at a time.

Mission

To see to the sustainability of animal life all round by providing rescue services, foster care and adoption in order to ensure animals are well placed in homes or our shelters.

Mission

To rescue the mistreated, save the injured, love the abandoned and bring awareness about a better understanding of where animals can be beneficial in our lives.

How we were birthed

Animaux came about in 2022. A group of tech women from around the world came together to find a solution to Sustainable Development Goal 15. The Sustainable Development Goal 15 which is about Life on Land was established by the United nations in 2015 to help promote, restore and promote sustainable use of the ecosystem. We are an African pet-loving community on a mission to create a better world for animals and to end animal cruelty and suffering forever. At Animaux, we are committed to providing help for animals to thrive in a world where they can't actually speak for themselves. To feel loved and protected, to matter too.
